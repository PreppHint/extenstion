Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns through Google Analytics
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To improve website functionality and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies and to delete them. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.
